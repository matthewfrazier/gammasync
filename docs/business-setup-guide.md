# GammaSync iOS Business Setup Guide

## Executive Summary
**Recommended**: Form Delaware LLC for business credibility and liability protection
**Timeline**: 4-6 weeks for complete setup
**Total First Year Cost**: $1,189-$3,899 (including Apple Developer fees)

## Business Entity Options Analysis

### Option 1: Individual Apple Developer Account ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED
**Pros:**
- Immediate setup ($99 Apple Developer fee only)
- No additional paperwork

**Cons:**
- Personal liability for app issues
- Cannot establish business credit
- Limited credibility with enterprise customers
- Cannot get D&B DUNS number as individual
- Tax complications for business income

### Option 2: Delaware LLC ⭐ RECOMMENDED
**Why Delaware:**
- Business-friendly legal framework
- Established precedent for app companies
- Flexible operating agreement structures
- Privacy protections for members
- Court system specialized in business law

**Setup Process:**
1. Choose LLC name (check availability at corp.delaware.gov)
2. File Certificate of Formation ($90 state fee)
3. Obtain registered agent service ($100-300/year)
4. Apply for Federal EIN (free at irs.gov)
5. Draft operating agreement ($200-1000 legal fees)
6. Apply for D&B DUNS number (free, 5-10 business days)

## Required Registrations & Accounts

### 1. Apple Developer Program
**Entity Type**: Organization (LLC)
**Cost**: $99/year
**Requirements**:
- D&B DUNS number
- Legal entity documentation
- Authorized signatory verification
**Timeline**: 1-3 business days after DUNS verification

### 2. D&B DUNS Number
**Purpose**: Business credit establishment, Apple Developer Organization verification
**Process**:
1. Visit dnb.com/duns-number/lookup
2. Verify business doesn't already exist
3. Complete application with:
   - Legal business name
   - Physical business address (not PO box)
   - Business phone number
   - Federal EIN
   - Primary business activity (Software Development)
**Cost**: Free (standard 5-10 business days)
**Expedited**: $500 (1-2 business days)

### 3. Business Banking
**Requirements**:
- LLC formation documents
- Federal EIN
- Operating agreement
- Initial deposit ($25-$100 minimum typically)
**Recommended Banks**:
- Chase Business Banking
- Bank of America Business
- Mercury (tech-focused online bank)

### 4. Business Insurance (Optional but Recommended)
**Professional Liability**: $300-$800/year
**General Liability**: $200-$500/year
**Cyber Liability**: $500-$1500/year (recommended for health apps)

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High Risk: FDA Regulation
**Risk**: GammaSync's therapeutic claims could trigger FDA medical device regulation
**Mitigation**: 
- Legal review of marketing claims
- "Wellness" vs "medical treatment" positioning
- Clear disclaimers and warnings

### Medium Risk: App Store Rejection
**Risk**: Medical app review is more stringent
**Mitigation**:
- Early compliance review
- Conservative marketing claims
- Robust safety disclaimers

### Low Risk: Business Entity Issues
**Risk**: Delaware LLC formation problems
**Mitigation**: Use established formation service
