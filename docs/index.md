## About

CogniHertz is an Android application designed for the personal exploration of rhythmic light and sound stimulation at 40Hz. Inspired by emerging neuroscience research, this app provides a sandbox for users to experience synchronized audiovisual patterns and observe their own subjective responses.

**This is not a medical device and is not intended for clinical or therapeutic use.**

## Safety Warning

This app produces rhythmic flickering light and sound. DO NOT USE if you have epilepsy, photosensitivity, or severe light-triggered migraines.
