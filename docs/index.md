## About GammaSync

GammaSync is an experimental Android application for 40Hz audio-visual entrainment research. The app includes mandatory epilepsy warnings and safety disclaimers.

**Important:** This app produces rapid flashing lights and is not suitable for individuals with epilepsy or photosensitive conditions.
