# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Introduction

CogniHertz ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and disclosure practices for the CogniHertz Android application.

## Data Collection

**CogniHertz collects NO personal data.**

The app operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track user behavior
- Access contacts, location, or other sensitive data
- Store data on external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services

## Local Storage

CogniHertz stores the following data locally on your device only:

### App Settings
- Selected color scheme
- Dark/light mode preference
- Audio amplitude level
- Background noise enabled/disabled
- Session duration preferences
- RSVP reading speed and display settings

### Session Data
- No session history is stored
- No usage statistics are collected
- All data remains on your device and is not transmitted

## Permissions

CogniHertz requires the following Android permissions:

### VIBRATE
- Purpose: Provides haptic feedback for button presses and interactions
- Data Access: No data is collected through this permission

### INTERNET
- Purpose: Allows loading reading material from URLs for RSVP mode
- Data Access: Network requests are only made when you explicitly enter a URL. No automatic data transmission occurs.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose any user data because we do not collect any user data.

## Third-Party Services

CogniHertz does not use any third-party services, including:
- No advertising networks
- No analytics services
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage or backup services

## Children's Privacy

CogniHertz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Because the app collects no personal information, it is compliant with children's privacy regulations.

## External Displays

CogniHertz supports connecting to external displays (USB-C monitors, AR headsets like XREAL Air). This connection uses standard Android display APIs and does not transmit any data beyond the visual output.

## User Control

You have complete control over your app settings:
- All settings can be changed or reset at any time
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored settings
- No account or registration is required

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Data Security

Because CogniHertz stores all data locally on your device, your data security is managed by your device's operating system security features. We recommend:
- Keeping your device OS up to date
- Using device encryption
- Using a screen lock
