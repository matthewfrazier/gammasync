# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 25, 2026**

## Data Collection

**GammaSync collects NO user data.**

This application:
- Does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- Does NOT track user behavior or usage patterns
- Does NOT collect device information or identifiers
- Does NOT use analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry services
- Does NOT require user accounts or registration
- Does NOT access the internet for data transmission

## Local Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to third parties
- Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

GammaSync does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms that could collect user data.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy accordingly.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository issues page.
